Intro

This style guide is developed by Blackstone Media in December 2016 to serve as a guide when building out and adding content to the new Span Tech website. Its purpose is to provide details on the colors and typography used on the website, as well as examples of fundamental design elements, their layout, and their place in the hierarchy of the website structure.

Elements are numbered in each of the screenshots on the following pages. Below each screenshot is a list of those numbered elements and instructions on how each should be formatted.

If you have any questions about the content of this guide, you are welcome to contact the Blackstone team at hello@blackstonemedia.com.
